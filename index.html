Our Journey

Our journey began in 2007 when our loving Papa, Mr. Narayan, together with our Mama, Mrs. Radha Narayan, decided that helping children live better lives is what they wanted to do for the rest of their own lives. Papa had already spent most of his life helping those in need. However, soon enough, he knew that simply providing children with a few meals or paying their school fees so that they could spend at least a year or two more in school just wasn’t enough.
